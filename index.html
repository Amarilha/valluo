Question? Answers.

How is Atlas different from other agencies?

Atlas represents a partnership with a team that not only possesses deep digital marketing expertise but also prides itself on its ability to adapt and innovate across a variety of industries, including the medical and legal fields.

Our approach is centered around understanding the unique challenges and opportunities within each sector we serve, allowing us to create customized strategies that resonate with your specific audience. We differentiate ourselves through a commitment to excellence, transparency, and results.

Our team stays at the forefront of digital marketing trends and regulatory changes, ensuring our campaigns are not only effective but also compliant with the latest standards. We believe in a data-driven methodology, where decisions are made based on analytics and measurable outcomes, ensuring every dollar of your marketing spend is optimized for maximum return on investment (ROI).

How do you ensure your marketing strategies comply with industry regulations?

We specialize in navigating the complex regulatory environments of the medical and legal industries, ensuring all our marketing strategies comply with HIPAA, bar association guidelines, ADA compliance and other relevant laws and regulations.

How long does it take to see results from digital marketing campaigns?

While immediate improvements may be noticeable, significant and sustainable results typically take 3-6 months, depending on the competitiveness of your field and the specific strategies employed.

How do Atlas track key performance indicators as it relates to our practice?

We utilize advanced analytics tools and methodologies to continuously monitor and analyze key performance indicators (KPIs) critical to your practice. By tracking metrics such as client/patient acquisition rates, cost per acquisition (CPA), and overall return on investment (ROI), we gain insights into the effectiveness of our marketing strategies. We then use this data to optimize campaigns, refine targeting, and adjust tactics to ensure we are not only meeting but exceeding your marketing objectives.

Our approach is transparent and collaborative, ensuring you have clear visibility into how our efforts are contributing to the growth and success of your practice.

How does align your marketing strategies with budget restrictions?

We align our marketing strategies with your budget by first understanding your goals and resources, ensuring strategies are both achievable and cost-effective. We focus on flexible, scalable tactics, starting with low-cost, high-impact methods to secure quick wins and demonstrate value. As results materialize and your budget allows, we then gradually scale up efforts, reinvesting in more comprehensive strategies to enhance performance and achieve your marketing objectives efficiently.
